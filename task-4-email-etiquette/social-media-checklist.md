# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## Do's
- Maintain an professional and respectful tone
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
- Think before posting any content
- Keep personal information private
- Verify information before sharing

## Don'ts
- Do not share sensitive personal details publicly
- avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Do not overshare your daily activities or location
