# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never click at suspicious links in emails or messages
- always verify the sender before sharing personal information
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Do not share OTPs or banking details with anyone

## UPI / Financial Safety
- Double-check UPI IDs before making payments
- Never approve unknown payment requests

## General Safety
- Keep your devices and apps updated
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
- Report cybercrime at https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930
